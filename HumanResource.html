What is a Human Resource Management System?

SA Human Resource Management System, or HRMS, is software that automates and manages the work in human resources; with this, the whole process of HR is made efficient. It can deal with a variety of functions such as recruitment, attendance, payroll, learning and development, and much more. VichaarLab's HRM Software provides a 360-degree solution in managing your workforce from hire-to-retire while giving you real-time insights to reduce administrative workload.
